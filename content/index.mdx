# Know Your Rights

Plain-language explanations of Louisiana education law for parents. Pick the
page that matches your situation:

- [Discipline & Suspension](/rights/discipline) — what schools must do before, during, and after any suspension
- [Charter School Rights](/rights/charter) — public records, open meetings, and budget transparency
- [Special Education & IEPs](/rights/sped) — your rights as an equal IEP team member
- [Records & Information Access](/rights/records) — how fast schools must respond, and what penalties apply

This site is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal
advice. Always verify with a licensed attorney for your specific situation.
